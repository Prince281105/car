Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas (kabuli chana) - 1 cup, soaked overnight
  • Onions - 2 medium, chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3, slit
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful

Method:

  1. Cook the chickpeas: Drain the soaked chickpeas and pressure cook them for 3-4 whistles, or until tender. Alternatively, boil them in a pot until tender.
  2. Sauté the spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the spices: Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder (optional). Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  5. Add the chickpeas: Add the cooked chickpeas to the pan and mix well. Add garam masala and salt. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the flavors meld.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice, roti, or paratha.
