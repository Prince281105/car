Malai Kofta

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) - 200 grams, grated
  • Potatoes - 2 medium, boiled and mashed
  • Green peas - 1/2 cup, boiled
  • Cashews - 1/4 cup, soaked
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Onions - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, pureed
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Cream - 1/4 cup
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - for deep frying
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful

Method:

  1. Prepare the koftas: In a bowl, combine the grated paneer, mashed potatoes, boiled green peas, soaked cashews (ground into a paste), ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Mix well to form a dough.
  2. Shape the koftas: Shape the dough into small balls (koftas) and set aside.
  3. Deep fry the koftas: Heat oil in a deep pan. Gently drop the koftas into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Prepare the gravy: Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Add the spices: Add cumin powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder (optional). Sauté for a minute.
  6. Add the tomato puree: Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the sides of the pan.
  7. Add the milk and cream: Add milk and cream to the pan. Simmer for a few minutes until the gravy thickens slightly.
  8. Add the koftas: Add the fried koftas to the gravy. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the flavors meld.
  9. Season and serve: Season with garam masala and salt. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
