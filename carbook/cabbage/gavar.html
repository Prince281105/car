Gavar ki Sabji (Cluster Beans Sabji)

Ingredients:

  • Cluster beans (gawar) - 1 cup, chopped
  • Onions - 1 medium, chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3, slit
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Heeng (asafoetida) - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (mustard oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful
  • Grated coconut - 1/4 cup (for garnish)

Method:

  1. Prepare the cluster beans: Wash and chop the cluster beans into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Sauté the spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add cumin seeds and chopped onions. Sauté until the onions turn translucent.
  3. Add the spices: Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric powder, and red chili powder (optional). Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  5. Add the cluster beans: Add the chopped cluster beans to the pan. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the beans are tender.
  6. Season and serve: Season with salt. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and grated coconut. Serve hot with roti, rice, or paratha.
