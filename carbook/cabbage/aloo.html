Aloo Methi

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes - 4 medium, peeled and cubed
  • Fenugreek leaves (methi) - 1 cup, fresh or dried
  • Onions - 1 medium, chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3, slit
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful

Method:

  1. Boil the potatoes: Boil the potatoes in a pot of water until they are tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté the spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the spices: Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric powder, and red chili powder (optional). Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  5. Add the potatoes and methi: Add the boiled potatoes and fenugreek leaves to the pan. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the flavors meld.
  6. Season and serve: Season with garam masala and salt. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice, roti, or paratha.
