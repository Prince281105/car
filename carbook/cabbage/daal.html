Dal Baati Churma

Ingredients:

  • Mixed lentils (dal) - 1 cup (moong dal, masoor dal, chana dal)
  • Wheat flour - 2 cups
  • Ghee - 1/2 cup
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Almonds (sliced) - 1/4 cup
  • Water - as needed

Method:

  1. Prepare the dal: Wash and soak the mixed lentils in water for 30 minutes. Drain and pressure cook them with cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder (optional), and salt until tender.
  2. Prepare the baati: In a bowl, combine wheat flour, salt, and a little ghee. Knead into a stiff dough using warm water. Divide the dough into small balls and shape them into round baatis.
  3. Bake the baati: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the baatis on a baking sheet and bake for 30-40 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Alternatively, you can bake them in a tandoor or over an open fire.
  4. Prepare the churma: In a pan, heat ghee. Add sliced almonds and sauté until golden brown. Add the baked baatis and crush them into small pieces. Add sugar and cook until the churma is well combined and slightly sticky.
  5. Serve: Serve the dal baati churma hot. The dal is typically served in a separate bowl, and the baati and churma are served together.
