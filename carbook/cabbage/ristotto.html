Risotto ai Funghi (Mushroom Risotto)

Ingredients:

  • Arborio rice - 1 cup
  • Mushrooms (like porcini or cremini) - 1 cup, sliced
  • Vegetable broth - 4 cups
  • Onion - 1 medium, chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • White wine - 1/2 cup
  • Parmesan cheese - 1/2 cup, grated
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Fresh parsley - 1 tbsp, chopped (for garnish)

Method:

  1. Sauté the aromatics: Heat olive oil and butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Cook the mushrooms: Add the sliced mushrooms to the saucepan and cook until they are tender and have released their moisture, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the rice: Stir in the Arborio rice and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, until the rice becomes translucent.
  4. Deglaze with wine: Pour in the white wine and cook until it is almost completely absorbed, about 1 minute.
  5. Add the broth: Gradually add the hot vegetable broth, 1 cup at a time, stirring constantly, allowing each addition to be absorbed before adding more. This process will take about 20-25 minutes.
  6. Stir in the cheese: Once the rice is cooked through and creamy, stir in the grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Season and serve: Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve the Risotto ai Funghi immediately.
