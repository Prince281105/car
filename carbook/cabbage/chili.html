Vegetarian Chili

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Onion - 1 medium, chopped
  • Bell pepper - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Kidney beans - 1 can (15 ounces), drained and rinsed
  • Black beans - 1 can (15 ounces), drained and rinsed
  • Crushed tomatoes - 1 can (28 ounces)
  • Vegetable broth - 1 cup
  • Chili powder - 2 tbsp
  • Cumin powder - 1 tsp
  • Paprika - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped onions, cilantro

Method:

  1. Sauté the vegetables: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and bell pepper and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic: Stir in the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add the beans and tomatoes: Add the drained kidney beans, black beans, and crushed tomatoes to the pot. Stir to combine.
  4. Add the spices: Stir in the chili powder, cumin powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Add the broth: Pour in the vegetable broth. Bring the chili to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes, or until the flavors have melded and the chili has thickened slightly.
  6. Serve: Ladle the Vegetarian Chili into bowls and top with your favorite toppings. Serve hot.
