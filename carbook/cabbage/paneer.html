Paneer Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) - 250 grams, cubed
  • Onions - 1 medium, chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, pureed
  • Bell peppers (capsicum) - 1 green, 1 red, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3, slit
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Cream - 1/4 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup (for marinating)
  • Lemon juice - 1 tbsp (for marinating)
  • Kashmiri chili powder - 1/2 tsp (for marinating)

Method:

  1. Marinate the paneer: In a bowl, combine the paneer cubes, yogurt, lemon juice, kashmiri chili powder, and salt. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill or bake the paneer: Preheat your grill or oven to medium heat. Thread the marinated paneer cubes onto skewers or place them on a baking sheet. Grill or bake for 5-7 minutes, or until lightly browned and cooked through.
  3. Sauté the spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add the spices: Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder (optional). Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add the tomato puree: Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the sides of the pan.
  6. Add the bell peppers: Add the chopped bell peppers and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until they soften slightly.
  7. Add the paneer: Add the grilled or baked paneer to the pan. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add the cream and butter: Add cream and butter to the pan. Simmer for a few minutes until the gravy thickens slightly.
  9. Season and serve: Season with garam masala and salt. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice, roti, or naan.
