ADADANI DAAL (URAD DAL)

Ingredients:

  • Urad dal (split black lentils) - 1 cup
  • Mixed vegetables (like drumsticks, carrots, beans) - 1 cup
  • Coconut (grated) - 1/2 cup
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/4 tsp (optional)
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (mustard oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Prepare the dal: Wash and soak the urad dal in water for about 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Grind the coconut: Grind the grated coconut with a little water to make a smooth paste.
  4. Cook the dal: In a pressure cooker, add the soaked urad dal, chopped vegetables, coconut paste, yogurt, turmeric powder, red chili powder (optional), and salt. Add enough water to cover the dal and vegetables. Cook for 2-3 whistles.
  5. Temper the daal: Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Once they splutter, add curry leaves and pour the tempering over the cooked daal.
  6. Serve: Serve the adadani daal hot with roti, rice, or paratha.
