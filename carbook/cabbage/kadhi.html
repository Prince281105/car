Kadhi

Ingredients:

  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Gram flour (besan) - 1/2 cup
  • Onions - 1 medium, chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3, slit
  • Fenugreek seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/4 tsp (optional)
  • Heeng (asafoetida) - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (mustard oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful

Method:

  1. Whisk the yogurt and gram flour: In a bowl, whisk together yogurt and gram flour until smooth. Add a little water if needed to achieve a thin consistency.
  2. Sauté the spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and fenugreek seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the spices: Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric powder, red chili powder (optional), and heeng. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  5. Add the yogurt mixture: Gradually add the yogurt and gram flour mixture to the pan, whisking continuously to prevent lumps from forming.
  6. Simmer and thicken: Simmer the kadhi for 5-7 minutes, or until it thickens slightly. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  7. Season and serve: Season with salt. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
