Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • Spinach (palak) - 1 kg, washed and blanched
  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) - 250 grams, cubed
  • Onions - 1 medium, chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, pureed
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3, slit
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Fenugreek leaves (methi) - 1 tbsp
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Cream - 1/4 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful

Method:

  1. Blend the spinach: Blend the blanched spinach with a little water to make a smooth paste.
  2. Sauté the spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the spices: Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder (optional). Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree: Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the sides of the pan.
  5. Add the spinach paste: Add the spinach paste and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the paneer: Add the paneer cubes and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the paneer is heated through.
  7. Add the cream and butter: Add cream and butter to the pan. Simmer for a few minutes until the gravy thickens slightly.
  8. Season and serve: Season with garam masala and salt. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice, roti, or naan.
