Kootu

Ingredients:

  • Mixed vegetables (like pumpkin, ash gourd, snake gourd) - 1 cup each
  • Moong dal (split green lentils) - 1/2 cup
  • Coconut (grated) - 1/2 cup
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Green chilies - 2-3
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/4 tsp (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (coconut oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and peel all the vegetables. Cut them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Cook the dal: In a pot, add the moong dal and enough water to cover it. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the dal is cooked through, about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Cook the vegetables: In a separate pot, add the vegetables and enough water to cover them. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Grind the coconut: Grind the grated coconut with a little water to make a smooth paste.
  5. Make the tempering: Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Once they splutter, add turmeric powder and red chili powder (optional).
  6. Combine and simmer: Add the cooked dal and vegetables to the tempering. Add the coconut paste and salt. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the flavors meld.
  7. Serve: Serve the kootu hot with rice or roti.
