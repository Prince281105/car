Vegetarian Philly Cheesesteak

Ingredients:

  • Portobello mushrooms - 1 large, sliced
  • Bell peppers - 1, sliced
  • Onion - 1 medium, sliced
  • Provolone cheese - 4 slices
  • Hoagie rolls - 2
  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

Method:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Slice the portobello mushrooms, bell peppers, and onion.
  2. Sauté the vegetables: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, and onion. Sauté until softened and slightly browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Assemble the cheesesteaks: Toast the hoagie rolls. Place the sautéed vegetables on the toasted rolls. Top with provolone cheese slices.
  4. Melt the cheese: Broil the cheesesteaks for a few minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Serve: Serve the Vegetarian Philly Cheesesteaks immediately.
