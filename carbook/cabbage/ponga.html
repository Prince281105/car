Pongal

Ingredients:

  • Rice - 1 cup
  • Yellow moong dal (split yellow lentils) - 1/2 cup
  • Ghee - 2 tbsp
  • Black peppercorns - 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Ginger (grated) - 1/2 inch
  • Cashews - 1/4 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Water - 3 cups (adjust as needed)

Method:

  1. Wash and soak: Wash the rice and yellow moong dal separately and soak them in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the pongal: In a heavy-bottomed pot, add the soaked rice, dal, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice and dal are cooked through and the mixture is thick.
  3. Temper the pongal: Heat ghee in a small pan. Add black peppercorns, cumin seeds, and grated ginger. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant. Add the cashews and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Combine and serve: Pour the tempering over the cooked pongal and mix well. Season with salt and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
