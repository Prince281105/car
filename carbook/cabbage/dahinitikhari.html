DAHI NI TIKHARI

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes (aloo) - 2 medium
  • Poppy seeds (posto) - 1/4 cup
  • Mustard oil - 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful

Method:

  1. Prepare the potatoes: Wash and peel the potatoes. Cut them into 1-inch cubes.
  2. Soak the poppy seeds: Soak the poppy seeds in warm water for about 15 minutes. Drain and grind them into a smooth paste.
  3. Sauté the spices: Heat mustard oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, asafoetida, and green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds until the spices release their aroma.
  4. Add the potatoes: Add the potatoes to the pan and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the poppy seed paste: Add the poppy seed paste to the pan and mix well. Cook for another 5 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the paste is well incorporated.
  6. Season and serve: Season with salt, red chili powder, and turmeric powder. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti, rice, or paratha.
