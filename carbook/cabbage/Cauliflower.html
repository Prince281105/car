Vegetarian Buffalo Cauliflower Wings

Ingredients:

  • Cauliflower florets - 1 large head, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Flour - 1/2 cup
  • Milk (or plant-based milk) - 1/2 cup
  • Buffalo sauce - 1/2 cup
  • Butter (or vegan butter) - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic powder - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste

Method:

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Coat the cauliflower: In a bowl, combine the flour and milk (or plant-based milk). Dip the cauliflower florets into the mixture, coating them evenly.
  3. Bake: Spread the coated cauliflower florets on a baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until crispy.
  4. Make the buffalo sauce mixture: In a small bowl, combine the buffalo sauce, melted butter (or vegan butter), and garlic powder.
  5. Toss the cauliflower: Toss the baked cauliflower florets in the buffalo sauce mixture until evenly coated.
  6. Serve: Serve the Vegetarian Buffalo Cauliflower Wings hot with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing (or vegan alternatives).
