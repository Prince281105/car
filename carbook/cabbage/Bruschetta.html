Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Baguette or Italian bread - 1 loaf, sliced
  • Ripe tomatoes - 2-3, diced
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Fresh basil - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Extra virgin olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Balsamic vinegar - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste

Method:

  1. Toast the bread: Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Rub with garlic: Rub the toasted bread slices with garlic cloves.
  3. Prepare the tomato mixture: Dice the tomatoes and combine them with chopped basil, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the bruschetta: Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted bread slices.
  5. Serve: Serve the Bruschetta immediately.
