SEV TAMETA NU SHAAK

Ingredients:

  • Sev (crispy noodles) - 1/2 cup
  • Potatoes (aloo) - 2 medium
  • Onions - 1 medium
  • Green chilies - 2-3
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (mustard oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and peel the potatoes and onions. Cut the potatoes into cubes and the onions into thin slices.
  2. Sauté the onions: Heat oil in a pan. Add the onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Add the spices: Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin seeds, and asafoetida to the pan. Sauté for a few seconds until the spices release their aroma.
  4. Add the potatoes: Add the potatoes to the pan and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the sev: Add the sev to the pan and mix well.
  6. Cook until done: Cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the sev is slightly softened.
  7. Season and serve: Season with salt and serve hot with roti, rice, or paratha.
