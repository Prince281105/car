RINGAN NU SHAK

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant (ringan) - 1 medium
  • Onions - 1 medium
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium
  • Green chilies - 2-3
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Coriander powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (mustard oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Prepare the eggplant: Wash and peel the eggplant. Cut them into 1-inch cubes.
  2. Sauté the spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and green chilies.
  3. Add the spices: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Add the eggplant: Add the eggplant cubes and cook, stirring occasionally, until they are tender and slightly browned.
  6. Season and serve: Season with salt and serve hot with roti, rice, or paratha.
