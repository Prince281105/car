Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • Fresh tomatoes - 2-3, sliced
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese - 1 ball, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves - 10-12
  • Extra virgin olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Balsamic vinegar - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste

Method:

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese. Wash and dry the basil leaves.
  2. Assemble the salad: Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating them with basil leaves.
  3. Dress the salad: Drizzle the salad with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Serve: Serve the Caprese Salad immediately.
