Cabbage Thoran

Ingredients:

  • Cabbage - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Coconut (grated) - 1/2 cup
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Urad dal (split black lentils) - 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2-3, slit
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (coconut oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Prepare the cabbage: Wash and finely chop the cabbage.
  2. Grind the coconut: Grind the grated coconut with a little water to make a smooth paste.
  3. Saut√© the spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves. Once they splutter, add green chilies.
  4. Add the cabbage: Add the chopped cabbage to the pan and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the coconut paste: Add the coconut paste and turmeric powder to the pan. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the cabbage is tender and the flavors meld.
  6. Season and serve: Season with salt and serve the Cabbage Thoran hot with rice or roti.
