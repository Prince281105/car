Vegetarian Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas - 6
  • Black beans - 1 can (15 ounces), rinsed and drained
  • Corn kernels - 1 cup, frozen or canned
  • Avocado - 1, diced
  • Lettuce - 1/2 head, shredded
  • Tomato - 1, diced
  • Salsa - 1/2 cup
  • Sour cream (or vegan alternative) - 1/2 cup
  • Cheese (or vegan cheese) - 1/2 cup, shredded

Method:

  1. Warm the tortillas: Heat the tortillas in a skillet or microwave until warm and pliable.
  2. Prepare the fillings: In a bowl, combine the black beans, corn kernels, avocado, lettuce, tomato, salsa, sour cream (or vegan alternative), and cheese (or vegan cheese).
  3. Assemble the tacos: Fill the warmed tortillas with the bean and vegetable mixture.
  4. Serve: Serve the Vegetarian Tacos immediately.
