Panzanella

Ingredients:

  • Stale bread (like ciabatta) - 1/2 loaf, torn into chunks
  • Tomatoes - 2-3, diced
  • Cucumber - 1, diced
  • Red onion - 1/2, diced
  • Bell peppers - 1, diced
  • Fresh basil - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Red wine vinegar - 2 tbsp
  • Extra virgin olive oil - 3 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste

Method:

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Tear the stale bread into chunks. Dice the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and bell peppers. Wash and chop the basil leaves.
  2. Combine the ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the bread chunks, diced vegetables, and chopped basil.
  3. Dress the salad: Drizzle the salad with red wine vinegar and extra virgin olive oil. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Let it sit: Let the salad sit for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve: Serve the Panzanella salad chilled or at room temperature.
