Avial

Ingredients:

  • Mixed vegetables (like carrots, beans, potatoes, drumsticks, pumpkin) - 1 cup each
  • Coconut milk - 1 cup
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Green chilies - 2-3
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (coconut oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and peel all the vegetables. Cut them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Cook the vegetables: In a large pot or kadai, add the vegetables and enough water to cover them. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Make the tempering: Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Once they splutter, add green chilies.
  4. Add the spices: Add turmeric powder and red chili powder. Saut√© for a minute.
  5. Combine and simmer: Pour the tempering over the cooked vegetables. Add coconut milk and salt. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the flavors meld.
  6. Serve: Serve the avial hot with rice or roti.
