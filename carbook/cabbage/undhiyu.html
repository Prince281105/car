Undhiyu

Ingredients:

  • Mixed vegetables (like potatoes, sweet potatoes, green beans, cluster beans, yam, brinjal, and pumpkin) - 1 cup each
  • Green chilies - 2-3
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (mustard oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp
  • Sugar - 1 tbsp (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) - a handful

Method:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and peel all the vegetables. Cut them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Sauté the spices: Heat oil in a large pot or kadai. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Once they splutter, add green chilies.
  3. Add the spices: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and asafoetida. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the vegetables: Add the chopped vegetables to the pot. Mix well and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add water and cook: Add enough water to cover the vegetables. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  6. Season and serve: Season with salt and add sugar (optional) to balance the flavors. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with roti, rice, or paratha.
