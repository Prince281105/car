Vegetarian Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, potatoes) - 2 cups
  • Vegetable broth - 1 cup
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Flour - 2 tbsp
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Salt and pepper - to taste
  • Puff pastry - 1 sheet

Method:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Sauté the vegetables: Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped vegetables and sauté until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Make the sauce: Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Gradually whisk in the vegetable broth and milk. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  4. Assemble the pot pie: Pour the vegetable mixture into a baking dish. Top with the puff pastry sheet, pressing the edges to seal.
  5. Bake: Bake in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  6. Serve: Serve the Vegetarian Pot Pie hot.
