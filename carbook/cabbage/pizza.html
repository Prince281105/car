Margareta Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade) - 1 ball
  • Tomato sauce - 1/2 cup
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese - 1 ball, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves - 10-12
  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste

Method:

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Prepare the pizza dough: Stretch or roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to a 12-inch circle.
  3. Spread the sauce: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edge.
  4. Add the cheese: Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
  5. Bake: Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Garnish and serve: Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve the Margherita Pizza hot.
