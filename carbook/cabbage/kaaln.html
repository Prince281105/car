Kaalan

Ingredients:

  • Raw plantains or yam (peeled and cut into pieces) - 1 cup
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Coconut (grated) - 1/2 cup
  • Green chilies - 2-3
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil (coconut oil or vegetable oil) - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Cook the plantains or yam: In a pot, add the plantains or yam pieces and enough water to cover them. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until they are tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  2. Grind the coconut: Grind the grated coconut with a little water to make a smooth paste.
  3. Make the tempering: Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Once they splutter, add green chilies.
  4. Combine and simmer: Add the cooked plantains or yam to the tempering. Add the coconut paste, yogurt, turmeric powder, and salt. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the flavors meld.
  5. Serve: Serve the kaalan hot with rice or roti.
