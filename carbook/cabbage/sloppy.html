Vegetarian Sloppy Joes

Ingredients:

  • Lentils (or vegetarian ground meat substitute) - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1 medium
  • Bell pepper - 1
  • Tomato sauce - 1 cup
  • Tomato paste - 2 tbsp
  • Brown sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Vinegar - 2 tbsp
  • Worcestershire sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Hamburger buns - 6

Method:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Chop the onion and bell pepper into small pieces.
  2. Sauté the vegetables: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and bell pepper and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the lentils: Add the lentils (or vegetarian ground meat substitute) to the skillet and cook for 5 minutes, breaking up any large pieces.
  4. Add the sauce: Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, brown sugar, vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Assemble the sloppy joes: Toast the hamburger buns. Spoon the lentil mixture onto the toasted buns and serve immediately.
